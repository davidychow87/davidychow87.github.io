  Static

  Gravity

- To throw ball: hold left mouse button - drag across screen - release left mouse button
- The faster you drag, the faster the initial velocity
- Use the 'Mass' slider to change the mass of the ball next ball
- Turn the 'Static' button on/off to make the next thrown ball not affected/affected by gravity
- Turn gravity on/off with the 'Gravity' button, this will cause balls to bounce off the walls!
- Change the gravity constant by pressing 'Page Up' and 'Page Down' on your keyboard
- Press 'Insert' on your keyboard to toggle between balls
- Press 'Delete' on your keyboard to delete the toggled ball
